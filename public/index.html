Our Services

The therapeutic process at Blooming Mind is tailored to each client. Most sessions are 55 minutes long and held once a week, but the frequency and duration can vary based on individual needs and preferences. We offer various types of therapy, including individual and family sessions, both in-office and via telehealth. The specifics of your therapy plan will be discussed and agreed upon with your clinician before starting.
