Services and Fees

Services Offered

The therapeutic processes at Blooming Mind are tailored to each client. Most individual therapy sessions are approximately 55 minutes long and held once a week, but the frequency and duration vary based on individual needs, preferences, and progress. Family therapy is offered in accompaniment to individual therapy when it is appropriate and requested by the client, parent, or therapist. Sessions are held in-person at the Jenks office or via telehealth (secure online video/audio call) depending on client preferences. We are committed to providing a safe, ethical, and supportive environment for each client. The consent and intake forms as well as specifics of your therapy plan will be discussed and agreed upon with your clinician before beginning services.


All our clinicians are master's-level licensed professional counselors (LPCs) or LPC-Candidates, trained in and practicing various approaches to mental health therapy. Depending on the client’s needs and preferences, the techniques, modalities, and theories used throughout the therapeutic process may include: Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), dialectical behavioral therapy (DBT), humanistic (person-centered, existential, gestalt) therapies, mindfulness-based approaches, somatic experiencing, polyvagal theory, sensory integration techniques, narrative therapy, attachment-based therapy, cognitive processing therapy (CPT), interactive maladaptive communication management, trauma-focused CBT (TF-CBT), strengths-based therapy, animal assisted therapies (AAT), choice theory, grief therapy, alternative communication techniques, accelerated resolution therapy (ART), acceptance and commitment therapy (ACT), and a variety of interactive play therapy and art therapy modalities.


We are passionate about helping clients with challenges in a variety of areas, including but not limited to: autism, ADHD, anxiety, depression, emotional and behavioral dysregulation, sensory processing difficulties, trauma, grief and loss, relationship difficulties, social connection development, communication difficulties, adjustment issues, stress management, divorce, perimenopause, aging, and family functioning issues.


If you are interested in learning more about our services or scheduling an appointment, please contact us.


We look forward to supporting you on your journey to flourishing.


Fees

Insurances accepted at Blooming Mind differ per provider, but include: Aetna, BCBS, Cigna/Evernorth, First Health, Health Choice, Healthcare Highways/90 Degree Benefits, Medica, United Healthcare (UHC), United Medical Resources (UMR), and Oklahoma Medicaid (Soonercare/ SoonerSelect) plans.


Eligibility and benefits are determined prior to the initial intake session. Clients are responsible for all deductible, copay, and/or coinsurance payments their insurance determines is applicable to their specific policy.


Self-pay rates range from $80-$165 per session, differing per service and provider. Self-pay rates are for clients without insurance, who are out-of-network, or who choose not to use their insurance benefits for therapy. Please contact us for these specific rates.


Therapists at Blooming Mind PLLC cannot and will not represent clients in court. Mental health records are protected by both state and federal laws, and therapists cannot disclose confidential client information to the court without a court order signed by the judge to release both specific and relevant information. Court appearances cannot and will not be made without a signed order from the judge. Subpoenas signed by anyone other than the judge will not be considered for court appearance due to psychotherapist-client privilege laws and ethical codes. A flat fee of $500 in addition to the hourly rate of $175 will be required for court appearances.
