Our Services

The therapeutic process at Blooming Mind is tailored to each client. Most sessions are 55 minutes long and held once a week, but the frequency and duration can vary based on individual needs and preferences. We offer various types of therapy, including:

  • Individual Counseling - A personalized approach to therapy, helping individuals work through personal challenges and mental health concerns.
  • Family Therapy - Strengthening family bonds, improving communication, and resolving conflicts for healthier relationships.
  • Group Therapy - Connecting with others who share similar experiences, providing mutual support and shared growth in a group setting.
  • Telehealth Sessions - Accessible counseling through secure online video calls, allowing clients to receive therapy from the comfort of their homes.

The specifics of your therapy plan will be discussed and agreed upon with your clinician before starting. We are committed to providing a safe and supportive environment for all our clients. Our therapists are trained in various therapeutic modalities, including Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT), and mindfulness-based approaches.


If you are interested in learning more about our services or scheduling an appointment, please contact us.


We look forward to supporting you on your journey to mental wellness.
