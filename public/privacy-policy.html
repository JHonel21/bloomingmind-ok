Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 04/12/2025

At Blooming Mind, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle the information you provide when you use the contact form on our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a contact form, we may collect:

  • Your name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Any information you include in your message

2. Use of Information

The information you provide is used to:

  • Respond to your inquiry
  • Provide information about our services
  • Schedule appointments or consultations (if applicable)

We do not use this information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

3. Confidentiality and HIPAA Compliance

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, please be aware that the contact form is not intended for transmitting sensitive personal health information (PHI). If you are a current or prospective client, we recommend contacting us by phone or through our secure client portal for matters involving PHI.

We comply with applicable privacy laws, including the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA), in handling and safeguarding client information.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. Your information may be shared internally or with trusted third-party services only as necessary to respond to your inquiry.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and you submit information at your own risk.

6. Your Consent

By submitting the contact form, you acknowledge and agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with the effective date updated accordingly.
